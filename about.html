    Welcome to Cyber Warrior

    A cybersecurity awareness program aims to educate individuals about the importance of cybersecurity and how they can protect themselves from cyber threats. By understanding basic cybersecurity principles and adopting safe online practices, people can reduce the risk of falling victim to cyber attacks and help create a safer digital environment for everyone.
    For common people, cybersecurity is essential because it safeguards their personal information, financial data, and privacy from cybercriminals. It ensures that online activities, such as banking, shopping, and communication, are secure and protected.
